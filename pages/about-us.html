Our Company

KLASSIK PREMIUM® was founded in 2010 in Barcelona, where it quickly grew into one of the top reatailers of fine wines and spirits in Europe. The online store opened its cellars to the Web in 2015.

KLASSIK PREMIUM®’s astounding growth is a result of international expertise and a careful selection of products that stand out for their quality, following season, availability, and tasting opportunities. Its focused catalog represents a range of French, Spanish and Portuguese wines, Champagnes, Whiskeys, Cognacs, Liqueurs. We are always looking for breakout products, making them available in advance to our customers thanks to the thorough connections we have formed over the years with our providers.

Our Range

KLASSIK PREMIUM®’s philosophy is simple–we only offer the best products in the industry. This allows us to maintain a mixture of big names and underrated findings, to maintain the portfolio fresh and propositive.

KLASSIK PREMIUM®’s orders are handled with the utmost care in storage and shipping, supported by quick turnaround times straight from our suppliers and manufacturers, to guarantee quality and origin. Whether an aficionado or a collector you are guaranteed to find something that suits your needs, with the assurance that if you see it on the site, it’s ready to ship.
