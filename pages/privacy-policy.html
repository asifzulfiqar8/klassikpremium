Cookies

A cookie is a small text file stored on your device to recognize your device when you visit again (e.g., login, language, font size). Cookies allow us to customize the website to your browser. They typically do not contain personal data and are not programs that can carry viruses.
We collect information to identify your device and customize the content on the website. If you disable cookies, some functionality may not work properly. You can modify your browser settings to disable cookies at http://minecookies.org/cookiehandtering.
